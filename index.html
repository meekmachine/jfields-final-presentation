Motivation

The goal of the project is to explore and demonstrate the application of Large Language Models (LLMs) for enhancing the emotional intelligence of virtual agents. This is vital for improving the interaction between humans and machines, especially in roles that require empathy and understanding, such as customer service and healthcare.
